Hello, World!

2017.02.19

Time Optimist (n.) - Someone that thinks they have more time than they actually do; someone that wants to see and experience everything; and someone habitually late as a result.

I’m Emma, a programmer/rookie drummer/rollercoaster junkie and now occasional travel blogger hailing from London.

In new places, you’ll find me geeking out on walking tours; haggling through markets; eating my weight in local delicacies; and making frequent stops to pet a dog or two. Hopefully my experiences will give you some inspiration and help you in planning your own adventures.

Thanks for reading!
